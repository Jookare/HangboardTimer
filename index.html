Privacy Policy

Hangboard Timer does not collect any of your personal information. We do not store or transmit your personal details, nor do we include any advertising or analytics software that communicates with third parties.

The only analytics data collected is through the Google Play Store, which may gather information such as app performance and usage statistics to help us improve the app. This data is aggregated and anonymized, and we do not have access to any personal information.

What Information Is Collected

Hangboard Timer does not collect any personal information or connect to the internet. Any data that is created exists solely on your device, and can be deleted by uninstalling the app.
